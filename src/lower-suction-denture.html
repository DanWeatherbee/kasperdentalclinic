What is a Lower Suction Denture? 

Using special impression and processing techniques, patients are able to achieve suction on the lower denture, just like the top denture! Until recently this amount of stability on a lower denture  has not been available with out the assitance of adhesive or dental implants. Does your lower denture lift, float or fall out?
